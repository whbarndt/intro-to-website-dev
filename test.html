Hot Plasma vs Cold Plasma

Hot Plasma

Hot plasma is a type of plasma where both the ions and the electrons in the medium are of similar and high temperatures. These types of plasmas would be equivalent to the plasmas found in much of the sun.

Cold Plasma

Cold plamsa is a type of plasma where only the electrons in the medium are of high order temperatures. This brings about very interesting and useful properties out from this plasma. Uses include sterilization and mircrochip manufacturing.
