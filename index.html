Welcome!

Hello! My name is Hunter Barndt and this is my webpage project. The design and functionality are still in progress and probably will be indefinitely. It has a very simple design at the moment but will hopefully look like a professional website sometime soon in the future! Thanks for stopping by!

Purpose

This website/web project is multi-purpose. Practically, it's a tool for branding myself and promoting my tutoring buisness. Personally, it's both a design/art project and a platform for promoting my passion for learning and education. I enjoy building things from scractch. Many people use templates or helper websites for creating their web pages (because that's the smart thing to do) but I wanted to attempt to create a good looking website only from code I've written. Essentially treating it as my own art piece.

Recently I've been really understanding the importance of education. It really is an obvious thing to say; who doesn't think education is important? However, the reasons, how they connect, and how they apply to today I've realized more and more recently. More is said on this page here if you are interested.
